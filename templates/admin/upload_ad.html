Ad Placement Guide
Scanner

Displayed during ticket scanning process. These have the highest visibility and engagement.

Results

Shown when users view lottery results. Good for informational ads.

Homepage

Displayed on the main page. Best for broad reach.

Video Guidelines
  • Video should be in MP4 or WebM format
  • Recommended resolution: 1280x720 (16:9)
  • Maximum file size: 50MB
  • Optimal duration: 15-30 seconds
  • Videos should be engaging and relevant to lottery users
  • Audio is enabled by default but can be muted by users
