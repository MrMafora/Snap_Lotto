Instructions
How Screenshot Management Works

The screenshot system is designed to maintain exactly one screenshot for each of the 6 normalized lottery types:

  • Lottery (from Lotto)
  • Lottery Plus 1 (from Lotto Plus 1)
  • Lottery Plus 2 (from Lotto Plus 2)
  • Powerball
  • Powerball Plus
  • Daily Lottery (from Daily Lotto)
Actions
  • Synchronize All Screenshots: Automatically detects and captures missing screenshots. This is the recommended action when screenshots are missing.
  • Run Cleanup: Removes duplicate screenshots while ensuring exactly one screenshot per normalized type remains.
Note

The system automatically runs cleanup daily at 4:00 AM SAST to maintain optimal screenshot storage.
