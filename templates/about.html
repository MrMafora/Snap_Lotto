About Snap Lotto

What is Snap Lotto?

Snap Lotto is a free ticket scanning platform that allows users to check if their South African lottery tickets are winners. Simply upload a photo of your lottery ticket, and our advanced AI-powered system will analyze it, identify the game type, draw date, and your selected numbers.

We compare your ticket against our database of official lottery results to instantly let you know if you've won a prize, and how much you could claim!

Supported Lottery Games

  • Lotto Plus 1
  • Lotto Plus 2
  • Powerball
  • Powerball Plus
  • Daily Lotto
