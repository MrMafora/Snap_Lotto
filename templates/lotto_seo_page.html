About SA Lotto

SA Lotto is South Africa's premier lottery game, operated by Ithuba Holdings under license from the National Lottery Commission. The SA Lotto draw takes place twice weekly on Wednesday and Saturday evenings.

SA Lotto Games Available:

  • SA Lotto - Main game with jackpots starting at R2 million
  • Lotto Plus 1 - Additional game with R1 million starting jackpot
  • Lotto Plus 2 - Bonus game with R100,000 starting jackpot

How to Play SA Lotto:

  1. Choose 6 numbers from 1 to 52
  2. Add Lotto Plus 1 and/or Lotto Plus 2 for extra chances
  3. Wait for the official Lotto draw results
  4. Check your ticket with our Lotto scanner
