Responsible Gambling

Play Responsibly

While the lottery can be exciting, it's important to play responsibly and within your means. Set a budget for lottery tickets and never spend more than you can afford to lose.

If you're concerned about your gambling habits or those of someone you know, contact the National Responsible Gambling Programme on their 24/7 toll-free helpline: 0800 006 008.
